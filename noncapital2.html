Bangor

Bangor is Maine's third largest city!

Known for its lively arts scene, beautiful waterfront, and charming downtown filled with unique shops and restaurants. Bangor also serves as a gateway to central and northern Maine, and the nearby Acadia National Park.

Did You Know?

Bangor is the home of famous author Stephen King. His house has become a popular attraction for fans, surrounded by a gothic wrought-iron fence with bat and spiderweb designs.
