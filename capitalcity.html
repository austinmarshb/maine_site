Augusta

Augusta is Maine's capital city!

Known for its views along the Kennebec River, it features stunning green spaces and gardens, making it an ideal spot for outdoor activities and checking at the golden dome of the State House.

Did You Know?

Augusta, Maine is home to the historic Old Fort Western, the oldest surviving wooden fort in the United States.
